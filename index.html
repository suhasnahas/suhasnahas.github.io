I am condensed matter/materials theorist, who work in the physics of "Dirac Materials" like graphene, topological insulators, weyl semi-metals and related topological phases of materials. The materials are quite intruging from physical, philosophical and technological aspects. From a physical point of view, these materials exhibit numerous novel and exciting phenomena whose physics (most of them of fundamental relevance) are yet to be understood. The Philosophical importance of these materials are due to their close connections with the concept of emergence (Thanks to Philip. W. Anderson and Robert Laughlin for their articles "More is Different" and "A Different Universe: Reinventing Physics from the Bottom Down" ) where their behaviour (quasiparticle excitations) follow directly from equations developed for elementary particles. Finally, when it comes to technological aspects, "Dirac materials" are considered as an ideal candidate for implementing a variety of emerging technologies of the post-silicon era; ranging from devices for overcoming energy/environmental issues to materializing the dream idea of quantum computing.

I am a recent graduate from the Department of Materials science and Engineering of IIT Kanpur(2013-2015) and currently working as a project engineer in the same department. My postgraduate thesis was on the "First Principles prediction of the structure of Amorphous Semiconductors using Evolutionary algorithms" under the guidance of Dr. Somnath Bhowmick and Dr. Anshu Gaur. Apart from my thesis, I was fortunate enough to enolve in exciting problems related to the physics of low dimensional materials in collaboration with Dr. Amit Agarwal from the Department of Physics, which helped me get an overview of the current research status in physics. Prior to my postgraduation, I did my undergraduation from the department of Mechanical Engineering in National Institute of Technology(NIT)(2009-2013), Calicut, and schooling from St. Antony's Public School and Junior College, Kanjirapally (2007-2009) and Infant Jesus Anglo-Indian Higher Secondary school, Kollam (1998-2007)
